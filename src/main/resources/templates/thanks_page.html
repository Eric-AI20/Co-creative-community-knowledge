Key contributors

Emma

Director of AG design consultants

Emma is an urban designer with a background in architecture; she completed her PhD at the University of Nottingham in the subject of social sustainability, engagement and placemaking. Mixing the worlds of practice, academia and local authority, In her position at Nottingham City Council, Laura developed the Design Quality Framework, the first application of the UK National Design Guide at a local level.

Olivia

Architect and Founder of TerraLupa

Olivia is an Architect and Founder of TerraLupa, a platform for ecological regeneration and re-establishing our connection to nature, encouraging design that promotes positive integration with local ecosystems and communities. She runs the Co-Lab Studio for AWW in Bristol and is a part-time Associate Lecturer at the University of West England.
