The key objectives are:

  1. To increase the research capacity of small to medium enterprises (SMEs) by linking with a PhD / Research Masters project
  2. To encourage SMEs to undertake research and recruit researchers
  3. To prepare and train individuals to contribute to research as professionals
  4. To support the development of key technologies in the Convergence Area of Wales
  5. To promote higher-level skills development
  1. The Rank Foundation operates a model of engaged philanthropy, combining intellectual, social and financial capital in order to maximise impact, whilst leveraging additional funding and support:
  2. Financial Capital: flexible funding, long term support
  3. Social Capital: networking through RankNet and our Fellowship
